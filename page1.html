Seven

    Python Retail Bot. Seven was design and developed solely by myself. It is a windows application that is designed to help a user aquire in demand products. In 2020 and 2021 because of Covids effect on the supply chain, certain retail items had a sudden drop in supply but demand remained the same. Because of this many items become impossible for a normal consumer to purchase, this is because the products would sell out of stock within seconds. One of the features designed into Seven was a product monitor, essentialy you are able to 'Monitor' for products. If these products come into stock you would then be alerted via a discord message. This would allow you to purchase the product quickly.

Seven also allowed for users to use proxies for different functionalities. This would prevent a users IP address from being blocked by the websites they were monitoring.
