NFTSuite

    NFTSuite is a tool for people who trade NFTs. The software allows you to rapidly bid and counter-bid on thousands of NFTs at the same time. While many of the current software options available are limited to one or two marketplaces, NFTSuite supports most marketplaces. With a gorgeous UI and smooth performance, NFTSuite is at the top of the food chain. Token bids, Collection bids, and Trait bids are all supported and work flawlessly. Offering a variety of price points, this software should be attainable for most users who want to enter the scene. Additionally, NFTSuite provides tools to help you monitor your favorite NFT collections, as well as an analytics page to track your spending and bids.


The application is developed with Python, while the website is crafted using Ruby on Rails and hosted on an Amazon Linux instance. Authentication is handled by a Python server running on AWS, and the database resides on RDS through AWS. Our build process makes use of Docker. Additionally, we have seamlessly integrated a payment system into the website.
